Miss Anastancia Ndhlovu is a holder of a Master of Science in Development Studies and a BSC (Hons) Degree in Human Resource Management (HRM) respectively. A qualified DACUUM facilitator with a Certificate in Labour Relations,Anastancia has leadership    experience spanning from Human Capital Development, International Relations,          Governance and in the Legislature.She is also an lnternational Speaker of repute on    Sustainable Development focusing on Environmental Sustainability,Youth Development and Gender Equality and inclusion.\
\
Having served in the Zimbabwe Government and the National Assembly of Zimbabwe as Deputy Minister of Tourism and Hospitality Industry from September 2015 to November 27, 2017 and as a Legislator from March 29, 2008 to January 10th 2018 respectively, Anastancia represented the Zimbabwe Government and the Parliament of Zimbabwe in many national, regional, continental and international meetings in general and Tourism and Travel engagements in particular.Overally she assisted the Minister of Tourism and Hospitality Industry in giving   political leadership and policy direction to the Ministry in line with the Ministry’s mandate as outlined by the Head of State.  Prior to the above mandate,Anastancia was the Chairperson of the Parliamentary Portfolio Committee on Environment,Water,Tourism and Hospitality lndustry and also the Head of the Parliament of Zimbabwe Delegation to the UN Climate Change Negotiations.This role saw her spearheading the formation of the GLOBE Chapter in Zimbabwe and subsequently becoming its inaugural President before her appointment into the Executive.She was instrumental in encouraging the Executive to review and come up with climate change and natural capital legislation and policies that help Zimbabwe to adapt to and mitigate against the effects of climate change and climate variability.

 

Anastancia Ndhlovu has travelled the breadth and length of the globe in pursuit of the  above and in the process gained vast experience in the travel and tourism sector at the highest level, that is the policy, or governance level through participating at various must attend and big travel and tourism expos such as  JATA in Japan, Akwaaba Travel Show in Nigeria, Durban Indaba, Pearl of Africa in Uganda and the CITM in Singapore among others.This also saw her winning many awards in recognition of her outstanding leadership skills and hard work.\
\
Born on 6th October 1980, Anastancia started her political career in 2000 and was a member of the Central Committee of the ruling ZANU-PF party from September 2009 to  November 2017. She also served in its Youth League National Executive from May 2004 to November 2017.  Prior to the national mandate she was in the Midlands Provincial Youth League Executive from December 2002 to May 2004. \
\
In January 2004 she joined the Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA) as a Human Resource Specialist based at the Head Office where she was instrumental in developing   Human Capital Management, Industrial Relations as well as Human Capital Development policies for the Authority which had just been set up in September 2001. \
\
In March of 2007 Anastancia took leave from ZIMRA after being seconded to represent Zimbabwe as Vice President of the World Federation of Democratic Youth (WFDY) in Budapest Hungary until May 2009. She was in the International Organising Committee of the 19th World Festival of Youth and Students hosted by Russia in Sochi in October 2017.Anastancia was the winner of the African Achievers Award 2019 Excellence in Leadership and lnternational Relations Award, the 2019 Peacful Mind Foundation Excellence in Empowerment Award, the 2019 World Habitat Ambassadors Foundation International Global Excellence in Empowerment and National Integration Award as well as the 2019 World Business Leaders Summit and Honors Special Recognition for her outstanding contribution and efforts to the world economy. She was also awarded thethe 2019 Young Entrepreneurs African lcon Award.ln July 2019 she was awarded the African Achievers "Excellence in Leadership & International Relations" Award in the UK House of Parliament. Over and above that, Anastancia was recognized as an Ambassador for Peace by the Peace Ambassador Agency on 21st September 2019, in Abuja,Nigeria. She also won the 2018 Karifest Continental Award for Women Empowerment. Anastancia also won the 2017 Africa Travel 100 Women Award, was Winner of the 2017 Mega Fest Women Outstanding Innovative Leader of the Year Award,2013 JCI Zimbabwe Ten Outstanding Young Persons (TOYP) Zimbabwe Honoree and featured several times in various Top 100 and Top 50 Outstanding Zimbabweans below 40 rankings 2012 – 2017 lists by the Gorindemabwe Frontier Institute.\
\
Anastancia is currently the Vice President of the Women Advancement for Economic and Leadership Empowerment in Africa responsible for Southern Africa. Prior to this mandate, she was its Country Coordinator for Zimbabwe. She is a God fearing young lady with Entrepreneurial skills and Director of Manna Ventures (Pvt) Ltd, a vehicle with interests in agriculture, advertising and Investor promotion, among others. Anastancia is also the Founder and President of Manna Foundation, a Trust in pursuit of gender equality and inclusion,   climate change adaptation and mitigation, Entreprenuership and Co-operative development, among others.
